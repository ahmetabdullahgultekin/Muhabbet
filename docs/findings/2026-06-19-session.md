## Multi-device linked sessions — RECOMMENDATION: PAUSE (do not build the non-crypto remainder now)
The non-crypto scaffolding shipped 2026-06-06 (flag OFF): data model `V18`, `DeviceLinkController`,
`DeviceLinkingService`, mobile `LinkedDevicesScreen`/`LinkDeviceScreen`. The remaining "buildable now"
slices are per-device delivery fan-out (`message_device_delivery`) and QR render/scan.

**Recommendation: do NOT implement those now.** Rationale:
- The entire **user-visible** payoff ("a second device decrypts and sees your messages") is **BLOCKED**
  on the libsignal re-integration (the standing crypto blocker — `DeviceLinkCrypto` is
  `NotYetImplemented` and cannot be faked with home-grown crypto: "do not guess crypto").
- Building delivery fan-out + QR scanning now adds schema + plumbing with **zero user value** until
  crypto lands — that is exactly the YAGNI/KISS the project's own rules forbid.
- It ships dark and prod is single-device; nothing is broken by leaving it.
- **Trigger to resume:** when libsignal is re-integrated + crypto-reviewed (unblocks Tier 1.1 / 2.4 /
  3.1), build per-device fan-out and QR scan *together* so the feature is shippable end-to-end.
